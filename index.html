A brash but brilliant inventor, Stark was self-described as a genius, billionaire, playboy, and philanthropist.

Captain Steven Grant "Steve" Rogers is a World War II veteran, a founding member of the Avengers, and Earth's first known superhero.

Thor Odinson is the Asgardian God of Thunder, the former king of both Asgard and New Asgard, a founding member of the Avengers.

Doctor Robert Bruce Banner, M.D., Ph.D., is a renowned scientist and a founding member of the Avengers.

Clinton Francis "Clint" Barton is an extremely skilled marksman, a former special agent of S.H.I.E.L.D. and one of the founding members of the Avengers.

Natalia Alianovna "Natasha" Romanoff was one of the most talented spies and assassins in the entire world.
